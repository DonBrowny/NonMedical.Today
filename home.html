    Guaranteed Plan

    Offer your clients ages 40 to 79 an exceptional combination of guaranteed protection with sum insured up to $50,000 in Specialty Life Insurance's Guaranteed Protection Plan. Under Guaranteed Protection Plan, applicants can qualify instantly for coverage as long as they meet the eligibility criteria for the plan. There are no medicals or health questions required for full coverage,and a policy may be issued the same day as your client applies.

    Eligibility
    There are two requirements to qualify for up to $50,000 in coverage under Guaranteed Protection: Your client is aged 40 to 79; and Your client has a permanent Canadian address, as evidence of Canadian residency. Your client may be eligible to buy this policy even if:
    • Your client has been deferred, postponed, or declined insurance for any reason;
    • Your client has past health issues that would lead to a deferral or decline under traditional plans; or
    • Your client is a newly landed immigrant or otherwise not a Canadian citizen
    Coverage
    Your clients can apply for $1,000 and up to $50,000 of coverage without any health questions.
    During the first two years of coverage, the policy benefit is equal to the sum of all premiums paid plus 3% simple interest. After this two year period, full benefits are payable equal to the sum insured.
    Benefits
    Guaranteed acceptance - There is no medical underwriting, ensuring all applicants who meet the age and residency requirements are covered.
    Coverage up to $50,000 - Industry-leading coverage amounts give your clients the flexibility to cover liabilities with a tax-free death benefit.
    No citizenship required - The life insured needs to be a permanent Canadian resident to qualify, making this plan a suitable fit for applicants without Canadian citizenship or who are proceeding on the path to Canadian citizenship.
    Instant issue - Specialty Life Insurance's fast application process can be completed entirely online, and you can provide your applicants with an issued policy in as little as one business day.

Our Critical Illness plan is particularly valuable for clients whom have already suffered from illness in the past and can understand the importance of protecting their lifestyle and future security.

    Eligibility

    Benefits

    Guaranteed even with Pre-existing conditions - Even if your client has suffered from one of the below conditions in the past, they are still protected in the event of any of the remaining conditions, with some exclusions.
    Second Event benefit - Your client can keep existing coverage in force for remaining conditions, even after a claim has been made. The Second Event Benefit is payable once only per policy.
    Choice of coverage - Qualify your client for more coverage under a simplified policy for up to $150,000 in total benefits, even if their health would qualify them as hard-to-insure under other plans.
    Instant issue - Arranging, filling and submitting a policy is fast, easy, and intuitive for you and your client. Most applications are received and processed in one business day.
    Standard Protection

    Combining substantial coverage of up to $300,000 with simplified underwriting, Standard Protection Plan is a permanent life insurance solution for residents of Canada between the ages of 18 and 70 who need lifetime insurance protection with a simple and fast qualifying process offered by Specialty Life Insurance. Coverage of up to $150,000 is available for clients between the ages of 71 and 79. Standard Protection may be available, even if your client is concerned that a health issue could lead to a declined insurance application.

    Eligibility
    There are three requirements to qualify for coverage under Standard Protection:
    • Your client is aged 18 to 79
    • Your client is a Canadian resident; and;
    • Your client is a Canadian resident; and;Your client is able to truthfully answer NO to 11 qualifying health questions.
    Your client may be eligible to buy this policy even if:
    • Your client has been deferred, postponed, or declined insurance for any reason;
    • Your client has past health issues that would lead to a deferral or decline under traditional plans; or
    • Your client is a newly landed immigrant or otherwise not a Canadian citizen.
    Coverage
    Your clients aged 18 to 70 can apply for coverage of any amount, from $1,000 up to $300,000.
    Your clients aged 71 to 79 can apply for coverage of any amount, from $1,000 up to $150,000.
    During the first two years of coverage, the policy benefit is equal to the total sum of premiums paid plus 10% simple interest. After this two year period, full benefits are payable equal to the sum insured.
    Benefits
    Simplified coverage - Only 11 qualifying health questions are required to receive coverage, with no questions pertaining to your client's family history or travel intentions. Lifetime coverage - Your client's coverage lasts for a lifetime with premiums payable until age 100. Premium rates will never increase during the life of the policy. No citizenship required - The life insured must reside in Canada to qualify, making this plan a suitable fit for applicants without Canadian citizenship or who are proceeding on the path to Canadian citizenship. Instant issue - Specialty Life's fast application process can be completed entirely online, and you can leave your client with an issued policy in as little as one business day.
    Preferred Protection Plan

    With first-day coverage, preferred rates, and simplified underwriting, Preferred Protection Plan is an ideal option for clients who want all the benefits of traditionally underwritten insurance alongside the ease and simplicity of a no medical plan.
    Applicants who aren't able to qualify for Preferred Protection can still qualify for either Standard or Guaranteed Protection provided they meet the eligibility criteria for the respective plans.

    Eligibility
    There are three requirements to qualify for coverage under Preferred Protection:
    • Your client is aged 18 to 79
    • Your client is a Canadian resident; and;
    • Your client is able to answer NO to 19 qualifying health questions.
    Your client may be eligible to buy this policy even if:
    • Your client has been deferred, postponed, or declined insurance for any reason;
    • Your client has past health issues that would lead to a deferral or decline under traditional plans; or
    • Your client is a newly landed immigrant or otherwise not a Canadian citizen.
    Coverage
    Your clients aged 18 to 70 can apply for coverage of any amount, from $1,000 up to $300,000.
    Your clients aged 71 to 79 can apply for coverage of any amount, from $1,000 up to $150,000.
    Full benefits are payable equal to the sum insured on day one.
    Benefits
    Simplified coverage - Your client must be able to answer NO to 19 qualifying questions.
    Lifetime coverage - Your client's coverage lasts for a lifetime with premiums only payable until age 100. Premium rate will never increase during the life of the policy.
    No citizenship required - The life insured needs to reside in Canada to qualify, making this plan a suitable fit for applicants without Canadian citizenship or who are proceeding on the path to Canadian citizenship.
    Day one coverage with instant issue - Specialty Life Insurance's fast application process can be completed entirely online with your client, and you can leave them with full coverage the very same day you meet with them.
    Final Expense Plan

    Specialty Life's Final Expense Plan works to qualify difficult-to-insure clients and prospective clients who are difficult to insure or may have been declined for life insurance coverage in the past. Under this plan, there's no barriers to entry (subject to age), and a fast application process means you and your client will receive an approved policy within the day that it's submitted.

    Eligibility
    There are only two criteria needed to qualify your client for full coverage under the Specialty Life Final Expense Plan
    • Your client is between the ages of 18 and 74 inclusive, and
    • Your client has a permanent Canadian address.
    If the above applies to your client, they are Guaranteed Accepted under the Final Expense plan and cannot be denied coverage. There is no medical exam, no fluid tests, no questions and no APS required to qualify for any available amount.
    This allows coverage for your client, even if
    • They've been denied or rated for other life insurance products
    • They have health issues that would preclude coverage
    • They have risks that would make it difficult to insure for any reason.
    Benefits
    Guaranteed acceptance - Regardless of your client's state of health, they are qualified for coverage under this plan. No medical exams - No waiting around for tests to see if your client qualifies. This results in better client satisfaction and a quicker approval of new applications. Simple and effective coverage plans - Up to $25,000 in guaranteed life insurance benefits are available to your client with no health questions or evaluation necessary. No citizenship required - All your client needs to qualify is to meet the age requirements and have a Canadian residence, making this an ideal option for newly-landed applicants. Instant issue - Arranging, filling and submitting a policy is fast, easy, and intuitive for you and your client. Most applications are received and processed in one business day. Accidental death protection - Your client also benefits from up to four times their stated coverage amount in additional accidental death coverage, included at no extra cost, until age 75.
